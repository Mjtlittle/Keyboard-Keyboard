The Keyboard Keyboard

by Joshua Little


Notes are arranged from left to right, top to bottom on the keyboard (not including the number keys). For example, to play an E4, press [d]. To go up a note from there (F4), press the key beneath it, [c]. Cutoff duration can be increased or decreased by pressing either [-] or [=].
